About us

Meeta Bharwada, is a culinary expert who has been running "Rasoii" her culinary studio for more than 20yrs now.
The studio teaches culinary courses ranging from elementary cooking for teenagers to restaurant style cuisines and
also specialty workshops for gourmet chocolates, workshops for students travelling abroad and more.
She aims to makes kitchen a happy space with delightful & easy cooking techniques. Also a co-founder of "Truffles"
Chocolate Studio which unleashed her creative & artistic side with cocoa. A successful Food consultant & Food Stylist
for many food establishments across India just proves her love for Good Food.
